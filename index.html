üìã MDoNER DPR Guidelines

Mandatory DPR Sections:

  • Context/Background (10%) - Sectoral policy and strategic importance
  • Problems Addressed (15%) - Baseline data and problem evidence
  • Project Objectives (15%) - Clear development objectives
  • Technology Issues (10%) - Technology choice and feasibility
  • Management Arrangements (10%) - Implementation structure
  • Means of Finance (15%) - Financial planning and budgets
  • Time Frame (10%) - Project timeline and milestones
  • Target Beneficiaries (5%) - Stakeholder identification
  • Legal Framework (5%) - Regulatory compliance
  • Risk Analysis (5%) - Risk identification and mitigation

Quality Criteria:

  • Data Accuracy: Factual correctness and reliability
  • Completeness: Presence of all required sections
  • Technical Viability: Feasibility of proposed solutions
  • Compliance: Adherence to MDoNER regulations
  • Budget Realism: Market-based cost estimates
